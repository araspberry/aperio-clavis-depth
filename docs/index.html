Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Aperio?
No. You can read the Bible and use Aperio as a guest. An account (Apple, Google, or email) is only needed to sync your prayers, notes, and bookmarks across devices.
How do I delete my account?
In the app, go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, prayers, notes, and bookmarks. You can also email us at support@aperiobible.app and we'll take care of it.
How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, enter your email and tap "Forgot password" to receive a reset link.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your prayers, notes, and reading history are never sold, never shared with advertisers, and never used for ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What is Clavis?
Clavis is Aperio's AI commentary engine. It offers scholarly and devotional insight on any passage you're reading.
